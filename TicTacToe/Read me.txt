this project based on the game Tic-tac-toe.
tic Tac Toe is a two-player game.
the two players take turns putting marks on a 3x3 board.
player 1 uses the "x" symbol.
player 2 uses the "o" symbol.
you can play as many times as you desire.

RULES:
1) player 1 start the first move.
2) player 1 and player 2 take turns making moves.
3) A player marks any of the 3x3 squares with his mark and their aim is to create a straight line horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
4) the winner player is the one who succeeded to create a straight line.
